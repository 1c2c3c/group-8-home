What are the causes of water pollution in Zhujiang?




The high economic growth



The investment in environmental protection failed to keep pace with the rapid economic advances. Therefore, Zhujiang has been highly polluted. Many of its tributaries are worse than the lowest national surface water quality standard and unfit as a drinking water source.





The domestic wastewater



The wastewater were discharged into the river system without treatment. Only a few larger municipalities where only a portion of the wastewater was treated. In 2005, about 55 percent of the wastewater in Foshan Municipality was treated, while only 22 percent of the wastewater in Jiangmen Municipality was collected and treated.
