Water Pollution in Zhujiang



Introduction of Zhujiang


  • Also called the Pearl river

  • Extensive river system in Southern China

Location


  • River source: Maxiong Mountain

  • Major province along Zhujiang:
    Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Foshan, Zhuhai, Jiangmen, Zhongshan, Dongguan, Hong Kong, four districts and counties of Huizhou and four districts and counties of Zhaoqing

  • River mouth: South China Sea

Physical characteristics


  • Its length is 2400 kilometer long

  • Third-longest river in China

  • The basin size is 453,700 km² (second large river by volume in China)

  • Its average discharge is 9,500 m³
